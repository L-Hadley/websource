Who are we?

CHICAS is a group of staff, research associates, and postgraduate students in the Lancaster Medical School, part of the Faculty of Health and Medicine at Lancaster University.

What do we do?

Our main research focus is on the development and application of novel statistical and epidemiological methods. We work on spatial and longitudinal aspects of statistics, and increasingly in spatio-temporal models.

Where do we apply this?

Our work has involved national and international partners, and has had impact across the globe. We have a special interest in the epidemiology of disease in developing countries. See our map for more info.

Interesting Links

We have a set of external links of interesting spatial epidemiology applications and disease maps produced by other groups.
