Jo Knight, Peter Diggle and Data Science MSc students are collaborating with Matt and Kelly Heys in the Royal Lancaster Infirmary on projects using real world data to address problems in NHS healthcare.

  • Frances Biggin is using hospital and GP data to look at patterns of stroke in relation to a number of factors including rural urban address.
  • Eleanor Hudson is using statistical modelling to predict the number of patients coming into Accident and Emergency departments.
