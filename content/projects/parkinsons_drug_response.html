Jo Knight worked on this project with Mario Masellis from Sunnybrook Hospital in Toronto and in partnership with the pharmaceutical company TEVA. Results from analysis of 692 participants of a drug trial showed that there was an association between response to the drug and genetic variant an individual would carry.
