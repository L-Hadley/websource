Jo Knight is the Academic Director at Lancaster for the Connected Health Cities Project. The North West Coast grouping is a collaboration between Lancaster University, Liverpool University, AIMES grid services and the Innovation agency. The aim is to use data to improve healthcare. At Lancaster we have four PhD students working on different aspects of this issue as well and MSc provision. We also have work force development and business engagement projects. Nick King is the Project Lead.
