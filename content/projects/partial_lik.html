--- title: Partial Likelihood for Space-Time Models description: Developing methods for space-time point process models people: - diggle - kaimi - rowlings links: - url: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1541-0420.2009.01304.x/abstract description: Biometrics Paper ---

Partial likelihood models for space-time point data model the ocurrence of events conditional on the past events. This occurs naturally in such diverse areas as infectious disease models or patterns of birds choosing nest locations on a finite habitat.
