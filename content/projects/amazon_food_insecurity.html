Together with colleagues from LEC, we are working on a project to model risk of flooding and drought on food production and distribution for cities in the heart of the Amazon region.

A Â£300,000 ESRC fellowship awarded to Luke Parry of LEC is funding development of statistical models of possible food production and supply problems along the Amazon river system. By combining geographical data on navigational routes and food distribution networks with real-time river flow and height data we aim to produce an early-warning system for urban vulnerability to food insecurity.
