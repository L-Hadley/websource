A number of CHICAS staff and students are working on data and models relating to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Jon Read, Jessica Bridgen, Chris Jewell and colleagues from the Universities of Florida and Glasgow produced a paper based on the early data from Wuhan.

Max Eyre has built a web application for plotting case counts and other time-series data from Johns Hopkins CSSE.
