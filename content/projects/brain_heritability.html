Jo Knight, Dr Mallar Chakravarty (Computational Neuroscientist, Brain Imaging Centre, Douglas Institute) and graduate student Sejal Patel have been examining the heritability of different structures in the brain. The work has been published in Human Brain Mapping. They are now working to use this information in a machine learning framework alongside for brain imaging phenotypes.
