--- title: Patterns of Stroke description: Investigating Stroke Factors and Predictive Modelling people: - knight - diggle ---

Jo Knight, Peter Diggle and Data Science MSc students Frances Biggin and Eleanor Hudson are collaborating with Matt Heys of the Royal Lancaster Infirmary.

They are using hospital and GP data to look at patterns of stroke in relation to a number of factors including rural or urban address. In addition they are using statistical modelling to predict the number of patients coming into Accident and Emergency departments.
