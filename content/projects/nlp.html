--- title: Using Natural Language Processing description: Identifying Gene Ontology Terms in Publications people: - knight tags: - genetics ---

Along with an intern from the University of Ottawa, Jo Knight has worked on a project to use NLP to identify specified Gene Ontology terms within publications. This allows researchers to develop new hypothesis about genes to study. Jo received Wellcome Trust Seed funding in August 2016 to expand this project and is working with computer scientists to develop a method to incorporate NLP results with association statistics to identify genetic risk variants.
