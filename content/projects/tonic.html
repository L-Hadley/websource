TONiC is a national study examining the factors that influence quality of life in patients with neurological conditions. The study is led by the Walton Centre NHS Foundation Trust with collaborators across 35 specialist neurological centres in the UK. It is one of the largest studies on quality of life in neurological conditions ever performed in the UK.

The study will investigate more than 30 factors, including fatigue, pain, depression and weakness, to create models which will be used as tools to provide personalised care, increase efficiency and improve patient forecasting.

More information can be found on the TONiC webpage.
