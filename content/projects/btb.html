--- title: Genetics of Bovine TB description: MRC-finded research into the spatial genetics of Bovine TB people: - diggle - taylor map: -4.40010, 50.56023 ---

Bovine tuberculosis is a major problem for UK cattle-farming, with especially high prevalence in the South-West of England. Describing the geographical patterning of different genotypic strains of the disease-causing organism contributes to our understanding of disease-causing mechanisms.
