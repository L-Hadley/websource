The Geostats Summer School for 2015 will happen at Lancaster from the 16th to the 23rd of August. This series of summer schools has been running for ten years, most recently in 2014 in Bergen, Norway.

The school brings together statisticians and developers in the open source geospatial field to deliver courses to students from across the globe.
