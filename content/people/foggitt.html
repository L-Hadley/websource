I am a PhD student in the CHICAS Research Group (Lancaster Medical School), supervised by Dr. Luigi Sedda, Dr. Manoj Roy and Professor Roger Pickup. My PhD research will focus on how to transform water, sanitation and hygiene infrastructure and practices within informal urban communities in Accra, Ghana, leading to sustainable and measurable improvements in health outcomes.

Previously, I studied MSc Water, Sanitation and Health Engineering and BA (Hons) International Development and International Relations at the University of Leeds, with a period studying at the University of Ghana.
