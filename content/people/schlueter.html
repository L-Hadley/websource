I am a postdoctoral research associate and my main interest is to aid medical and public health research and to ultimately help improve people's lives. In order to do so, my focus has changed from biomathematics to statistics. I have developed a particular interest in longitudinal data analysis, survival analysis and joint modelling but I am generally excited about learning and developing different statistical methods in order to be able to address various questions of clinical importance or of interest to public health.
