I am a PhD student in Statistics at the Department of Statistical Sciences of the University of Padova (Italy) and I am currently a visiting student in CHICAS (Lancaster Medical School) supervised by Prof. Peter J. Diggle.

My main research topic is Spatial Statisics. I am investigating the effects of positional errors on geostatistical models. My interests fall also in other fields such as Statistical Learning and Biostatistics.

I obtained my B.Sc. in Economics and my M.Sc. in Finance from the University of Trento (Italy). For my master project I have conducted a spatio-temporal analysis of crimes in the municipality of Trento.

I love programming in R and I have recently published a blog about applied statistics with R
