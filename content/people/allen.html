Left CHICAS in June 2015

I am a Senior Research Associate in the CHICAS group. My project is the evaluation of public health policies aimed at improving heart disease rates across all socio-economic groups in the UK. I collaborate closely with the Department of Public Health & Policy at the University of Liverpool, as part of the LiLaC collaboration.
