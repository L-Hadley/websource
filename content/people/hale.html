--- username: halea title: Alison Hale description: Dr Alison Hale status: pdra --- Recently I have moved to the CHICAS group in the Lancaster Medical School - my background is in theoretical physics. My current research interests include developing statistical models and software for real-time surveillance of routinely recorded diseases in the veterinary sector. In addition, I have an interest in developing statistical models and software for estimating disease risk of a population travelling through a geographical region in which there are small pockets of pollution posing a health risk; the population are tracked by global positioning systems (GPS).
