--- title: Juan Escamilla Molgora description: Juan Escamilla Molgora status: phd --- My PhD research focuses on finding invariants in spatio-temporal relationships (networks) between biological species using datasets collected from different sources, sample methods and uncertainties. I'm interested in understanding how ecological relationships are dependent on external biophysical variables (continuous and periodic). The research involves the development of tools for handling and analysing remote sensed data and big (maybe not too big) databases. I work with Open Source and Free Software tools.
