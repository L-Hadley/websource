Opportunities for Graduate Study with CHICAS

MRes Global Health: Translational and Quantitative Skills

The MRes Global Health is designed to develop your skills in quantitative and translational research. It will prepare you to meet international demands for cutting-edge science in health programmes for vulnerable communities world-wide.

For full details including fees, funding, and applications see the University Postgraduate Courses page.

PhD Statistics and Epidemiology

A CHICAS PhD Doctorate is three years of full-time research working on a statistical and computational project, developing methodology and/or an application in spatial statistics, longitudinal data, or genetics. Many of our current projects highlight the work of our PhD students.

For full details about funding and applications see the University PhD Schemes page.

LSTM & LU MRC Doctoral Training Partnership PhD

CHICAS also supervises students admitted to the Liverpool School of Tropical Medicine and Lancaster University DTP. Applications for entry in 2018 are closed but watch the LSTM & Lancaster University DTP site for the next admissions round.
